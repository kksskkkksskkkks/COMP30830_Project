How It Works

Full FAQ ›
Step 1
🎫

Get a Subscription

Sign up for an Annual Subscription (€35/year) at dublinbikes.ie, or grab a 3-Day Visitor Pass (€5) at any docking terminal.

Step 2
📍

Find a Station

Use the map above to locate a station near you with available bikes. Check real-time availability before heading over.

Step 3
🔓

Unlock & Ride

At the terminal, enter your card number and PIN. Pull out the bike from the indicated dock. The first 30 minutes are free — return to any station when done.

Step 4
🔒

Return the Bike

Push the bike firmly into any empty dock until the green light confirms it's locked. Use the map to check for free docks before you arrive.
